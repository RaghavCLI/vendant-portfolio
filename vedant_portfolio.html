Who I Am

About Me

I'm Vedant Chaturvedi, an SAP specialist based in Ratlam, Madhya Pradesh, India. With a strong foundation in Computer Science Engineering from Sri Aurobindo Institute of Technology (2009–2013), I built a career at the intersection of enterprise technology and modern UI development.

From 2014 to 2019, I worked as an SAP Consultant where I designed and delivered SAP Fiori and SAPUI5 solutions for enterprise clients. After consulting, I transitioned into a full-time training role — channelling over a decade of expertise into helping developers master SAP's modern development stack.

Today I train professionals and students in SAP Fiori, SAPUI5, the RESTful Application Programming model (RAP), and Cloud Application Programming Model (CAPM) — technologies that define the future of SAP development.
